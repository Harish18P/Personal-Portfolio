A Verse from the Collection

Awakening…

Awakening…

Her hands move in snail pace,
as her eyes gradually sees light...
After nine long years she arises,
she witnesses a man with a grin on his face,
whose future was revived from the night...

Nine long years of his life were lost...
as he was praying like a sage in penance.

Awakening…

As, he tried to hold her hands to console her
she snatched her hand back in fear...
Trying to reminisce through the blur,
Alas, little did she remember,
that these were the hands she held on to, when in fear…

His eyes shed tears as he moves distant from her,
as distant as him in his beloved’s memory…

Awakening…

The Entire room had expected these blows,
but the prince was willing to cross oceans for his princess…
The Oblivious sleeping beauty just glimpses,
as a beacon of hope, a smile she throws,
letting him know that he is never kinless…

The sage had penanced for nine dreaded years, People wondered
how long before the eternal boon of happiness is granted??
